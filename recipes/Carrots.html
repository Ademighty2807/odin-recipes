Lemon-Glazed Carrots

Home

Very easy and quick to make!

Ingredients

  • Carrot
  • Butter
  • Lemon
  • Suger
  • Pepper
  • salt
  1. 2 carrots, sliced 1/4-inch thick
  2. 1 tablespoon butter
  3. 1 tablespoon brown sugar
  4. 1 teaspoon lemon juice
  5. 1 pinch salt and ground black pepper to taste
